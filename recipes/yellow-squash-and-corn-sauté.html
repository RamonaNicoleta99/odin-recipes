Yellow Squash and Corn Sauté

Description

Yellow Squash Corn Saute is a simple, flavorful, and delectable summer dish. The dish features fresh sweet corn sauteed with onion, garlic, sun-ripened cherry tomatoes, buttery yellow squash, and basil, making it the perfect side dish for any meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 ears corn, husked and cleaned
  • 2 yellow squash, diced
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons butter, or more to taste
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Step 1: Gather all ingredients.
  2. Step 2: Place corn into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, 10 to 15 minutes. Drain and set aside to cool.
  3. Step 3: While the corn is cooling, place squash into a skillet and add water. Cook over medium heat until tender, 5 to 10 minutes. Drain any excess water from the skillet.
  4. Step 4: Cut kernels off the cobs. Stir corn, butter, parsley, salt, and pepper into squash; cook, stirring occasionally, until heated through, about 5 minutes.
