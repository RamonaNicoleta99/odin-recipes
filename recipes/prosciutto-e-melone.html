Prosciutto e Melone

Home

Description

Prosciutto e melone is a classic Italian appetizer featuring prosciutto wrapped around slices of cantaloupe, originates from Italy.

Ingredients

    1 cantaloupe - seeded and cut into 8 wedges
    8 thin slices prosciutto

Steps

    Step 1: Remove the flesh from the rind of the cantaloupe
    Step 2: wrap each piece of cantaloupe with a slice of prosciutto
